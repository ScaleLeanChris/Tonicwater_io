üí° How to Choose the Right Tonic

For Juniper-Forward Gins

Classic London Dry gins like Tanqueray and Beefeater pair best with Fever-Tree Indian Tonic or Q Tonic. The clean, crisp quinine complements the bold juniper notes without overpowering them.

For Floral & Botanical Gins

Gins like Hendrick's or The Botanist work beautifully with Fever-Tree Elderflower or Fever-Tree Mediterranean. The subtle botanicals in these tonics enhance the gin's complexity.

For Citrus-Forward Gins

Tanqueray Rangpur or citrus-heavy gins shine with Schweppes or Canada Dry for a refreshing, zesty combination. Add a wheel of lime to complete the experience.

Budget-Friendly Options

Don't underestimate Schweppes - it's been perfecting tonic since the 1870s. For a step up without breaking the bank, Canada Dry offers excellent value with crisp carbonation.
